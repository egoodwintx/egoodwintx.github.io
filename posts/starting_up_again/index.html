Starting up again

After a two year hiatus, I’m back to chronicling my thoughts online. I’ve been thinking alot about the early days of the internet and how much fun it was to be a part of it. Blogrolls and RSS feeds and just feeling like you could roll your own blog software and make something that was functional.

Maybe its all the AI talk about how content’s not going to matter anymore, because the bots are going to just autogenerate it for you now. I’ve always liked an underdog story, so maybe its time to revive human-generated content at the tail end of the human internet. At the very least it will be training material for the machines :)
